Our Story

About Us

_ _ _

We started Prathibha Solutions in October 2023 as a small startup with big dreams. Our cofounders, Shravan Kumar and Aakruthi, began by creating websites for local businesses. We saw how much a good website could help businesses get more customers.

After building websites and seeing great results for our clients, we expanded into mobile apps, graphic design, and digital marketing. Each new service came from listening to what our customers actually needed to grow their business.

What makes us different? We keep things simple. Instead of using complicated tech words, we talk to you like normal people. We understand that most business owners just want technology that helps them get more customers and grow their business.

We're still a young company, but we're passionate about what we do. Every website we build, every app we create, and every marketing campaign we run is designed to help your business succeed. We treat every project like it's our own business.

What We Believe In

  • Keep It Simple: We explain everything in plain English and build solutions that are easy to use.
  • Real Results: We focus on what actually helps your business grow, not just fancy features.
  • Always Learning: Technology changes fast, so we keep learning new things to help you better.
  • Fair Pricing: Good technology shouldn't cost a fortune. We offer honest, affordable prices.

Why Choose Us?

Since starting in October 2023, we've helped many businesses create their online presence. We're not the biggest company, but we care about every single project. When you work with us, you're not just another client - you're part of our growing family.

Here's what you can expect when you work with Prathibha Solutions:

  • Affordable prices that won't break your budget
  • We actually listen to what you need
  • Quick response when you have questions
  • We speak normal English, not tech jargon
  • Your project gets personal attention from our founders
  • We know the latest technologies and trends
  • Support even after your project is done
  • Honest advice about what will work for your business

Ready to grow your business online? Let's talk about how we can help.
